DR.BHIMA RAJU AMBEDKAR(1891-1956)

~"FATHER OF MODERN INDIA"

About

Dr. Bhimrao Ramji Ambedkar, popularly known as Babasaheb Ambedkar, was an Indian jurist, economist, social reformer, and politician. He played a significant role in India’s struggle for independence and the drafting of its constitution. Ambedkar inspired the Dalit Buddhist Movement and campaigned against social discrimination against Untouchables (Dalits). He is also known as the Father of the Indian Constitution.

Achievements

  • 1.He was the chief architect of the Indian Constitution.

  • 2.He fought against untouchability and caste discrimination.

  • 3.He played a key role in establishing the Reserve Bank of India.

  • 4.He worked for the rights and upliftment of Dalits and women.

  • 5.He embraced Buddhism and inspired millions to follow it.
